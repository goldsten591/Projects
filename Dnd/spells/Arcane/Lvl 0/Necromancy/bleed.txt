School necromancy; Level: cleric/oracle 0,  inquisitor 0,  psychic 0,  shaman 0,  sorcerer/wizard 0,  witch 0CASTINGCasting Time 1 standard actionComponents V, SEFFECTRange close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)Target one living creatureDuration instantaneousSaving Throw Will negates; Spell Resistance yesDESCRIPTIONYou cause a living creature that is below 0 hit points but stabilized to resume dying. Upon casting this spell, you target a living creature that has -1 or fewer hit points. That creature begins dying, taking 1 point of damage per round. The creature can be stabilized later normally. This spell causes a creature that is dying to take 1 point of damage.
