School necromancy; Level: inquisitor 0,  magus 0,  sorcerer/wizard 0CASTINGCasting Time 1 standard actionComponents V, SEFFECTRange close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)Effect rayDuration instantaneousSaving Throw none; Spell Resistance yesDESCRIPTIONYou direct a ray of positive energy.You must make a ranged touch attack to hit, and if the ray hits an undead creature, it deals 1d6 points of damage to it.
