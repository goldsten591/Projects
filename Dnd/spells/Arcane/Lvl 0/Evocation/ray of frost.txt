School evocation [cold]; Level: magus 0,  sorcerer/wizard 0;  Elemental School: water 0CASTINGCasting Time 1 standard actionComponents V, SEFFECTRange close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)Effect rayDuration instantaneousSaving Throw none; Spell Resistance yesDESCRIPTIONA ray of freezing air and ice projects from your pointing finger. You must succeed on a ranged touch attack with the ray to deal damage to a target. The ray deals 1d3 points of cold damage.
