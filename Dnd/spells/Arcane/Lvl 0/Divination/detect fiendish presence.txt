School divination; Level bard 0, cleric 0, magus 0, sorcerer/wizard 0, witch 0CASTINGCasting Time 1 standard actionComponents V, SEFFECTRange 60 ft.Area cone-shaped emanationDuration concentration, up to 10 minutes/level (D)Saving Throw none; Spell Resistance noDESCRIPTIONThis spell functions like detect evil, except that it detects only outsiders with the evil subtype, as well as the lingering effects caused by their gifts, presence, and spells. It can also detect clerics and paladins of fiendish deities, including Asmodeus, archdevils, daemonic harbingers, and demon lords.
