School illusion (glamer); Level: bard 0,  psychic 0,  sorcerer/wizard 0CASTINGCasting Time 1 standard actionComponents SEFFECTRange PersonalTarget YouDuration 1 round/level (D)DESCRIPTIONYou surround yourself with disturbing illusions, making you look and sound like a bizarre, insane fey creature. You gain DR 1/cold iron against a single opponent until the end of the spell, or until you take damage.
