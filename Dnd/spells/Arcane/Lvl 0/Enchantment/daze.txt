School enchantment (compulsion) [mind-affecting]; Level: bard 0,  inquisitor 0,  magus 0,  psychic 0,  shaman 0,  sorcerer/wizard 0,  summoner/unchained summoner 0,  witch 0CASTINGCasting Time 1 standard actionComponents V, S, M (a pinch of wool or similar substance)EFFECTRange close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)Target one humanoid creature of 4 HD or lessDuration 1 roundSaving Throw Will negates; Spell Resistance yesDESCRIPTIONThis spell clouds the mind of a humanoid creature with 4 or fewer Hit Dice so that it takes no actions. Humanoids of 5 or more HD are not affected. A dazed subject is not stunned, so attackers get no special advantage against it. After a creature has been dazed by this spell, it is immune to the effects of this spell for 1 minute.Dazed Condition: The creature is unable to act normally. A dazed creature can take no actions, but has no penalty to AC.
