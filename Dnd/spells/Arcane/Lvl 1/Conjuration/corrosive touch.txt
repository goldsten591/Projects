School conjuration (creation) [acid]; Level bloodrager 1, magus 1, sorcerer/ wizard 1, summoner/unchained summoner 1CASTINGCasting Time 1 standard actionComponents V, SEFFECTRange touchTarget creature or object touchedDuration instantaneousSaving Throw none; Spell Resistance yesDESCRIPTIONYour successful melee touch attack deals 1d4 points of acid damage per caster level (maximum 5d4).
