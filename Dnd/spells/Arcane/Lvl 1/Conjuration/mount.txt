School conjuration (summoning); Level bloodrager 1, magus 1, sorcerer/wizard 1, summoner/unchained summoner 1, witch 1CASTINGCasting Time 1 roundComponents V, S, M (a bit of horse hair)EFFECTRange close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)Effect one mountDuration 2 hours/level (D)Saving Throw none; Spell Resistance noDESCRIPTIONYou summon a light horse or a pony (your choice) to serve you as a mount. The steed serves willingly and well. The mount comes with a bit and bridle and a riding saddle.
