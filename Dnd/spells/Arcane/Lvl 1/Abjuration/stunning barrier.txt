School abjuration; Level cleric 1, inquisitor 1, paladin 1, psychic 1, sorcerer/wizard 1CASTINGCasting Time 1 standard actionComponents V, SEFFECTRange personalTarget youDuration 1 round/level or until dischargedSaving Throw none and Will negates (see text); Spell Resistance no and yes (see text)DESCRIPTIONYou are closely surrounded by a barely visible magical field. The field provides a +1 deflection bonus to AC and a +1 resistance bonus on saves. Any creature that strikes you with a melee attack is stunned for 1 round (Will negates). Once the field has stunned an opponent, the spell is discharged.
